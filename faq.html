Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to Dr.Obote College, Boroboro?

Visit our admissions page and complete the online application form. You can also apply in person at our main campus. Located at the Hill in Boroboro, Lira City East Division?

What documents are required?

You will need your previous academic assessment( reports), Parent National ID/passport, and a completed Medicial form.

Which Combinations are offered?

We offer Both science and Arts combinations with an expirence of better teachers.

Our goal is to help every student to achieve their dream.

Are scholarships/Bursaries available?

Yes, DOC offers merit-based and need-based bursaries. See our financial aid office for eligibility.

What is College life like?

College life includes clubs, sports, events, and student leadership opportunities. We strive to make learning engaging and fun.
